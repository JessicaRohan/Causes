Save oil spill-affected penguins with adorable knit fabrics!

Penguins rescued from habitats destroyed by oil spills are often too ill to undergo the stress of being cleaned right away. Sweaters help keep the penguins warm, while preventing them from ingesting the toxic oil through preening. Eventually, the penguins regain their strength enough to be cleaned off & released back into the wild!
